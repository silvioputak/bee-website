About us

Our beekeeping adventure, fueled by incredible persistence, today includes over 100 bee colonies located in LR hives. Every day we draw new knowledge from beekeeping literature, share experiences with fellow beekeepers and learn from our own failures.
Our apiaries are constructed as mobile containers, enabling us to move hives throughout the Republic of Croatia.
In our work with bees, the whole family is involved, and it is the place where we connect with nature and with people. We also regularly participate in exhibitions, using tastings to bring our products closer to their excellence. Although we have been practicing beekeeping for years, we humbly admit that we are eternal students of this craft. We strive to constantly improve, strive and strive to achieve greater expertise in order to bring people as close as possible to the magic of life with bees, the benefits of their products and the healing properties they provide.

Honey strengthens your immunity, and good immunity

Honey contains antibacterial and antioxidant properties, helps to cleanse the body and has a good effect on the digestive system.
